Preserve your life: Essentials

When traveling to Brazil or interacting with Portuguese speakers, it's essential to know some key phrases that can help you navigate dangerous situations.

Introduction

While we hope you never find yourself in such circumstances, being prepared can make all the difference. This guide will introduce you to essential words and expressions in Brazilian Portuguese that could prove invaluable in emergencies. From asking for help to communicating with authorities, these phrases will empower you to react quickly and effectively, ensuring your safety and the safety of those around you. Dive in and equip yourself with the knowledge that could be a lifesaver.

Essentials

Ajuda | Help
Médico | Doctor
Policial | Cop
Bombeiro | Fire-fighter
Fogo | Fire
Consulta | Appointment
Notícias | News

It is very important that you review these words, in stressful situations we may end up forgetting the words, so please review and study a little more so that you can be safer!
