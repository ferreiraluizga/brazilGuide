WH words and decision

These interrogatives are crucial in everyday conversations, academic inquiries, and professional settings, enabling effective communication and comprehension.

Introduction

In this content, we will delve into the fundamental questions that are essential for understanding any situation or event: who, what, where, and when. These questions help establish the context and provide clarity on the various aspects of the subject matter. Additionally, we will explore decision-making through yes-or-no questions, which can simplify complex scenarios and assist in making informed choices. This guide will be presented in Brazilian Portuguese, making it accessible and relevant for those familiar with the language and culture. Let's begin our journey of inquiry and decision-making with the following sections:

Decision

Sim | Yes
Não | No

WH words

Quem? | Who?
O que? | What?
Quando? | When?
Where? | What?

Quem foi para o mercado?
Who went to the market?
O que você estava vendo?
What were you seeing?
Quando isso vai acontecer?
When is it going to happen?

By addressing these questions, we aim to provide a comprehensive understanding that empowers you to analyze and decide effectively in various contexts, congratulations!
