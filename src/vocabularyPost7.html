Enjoy your stay: Purchase

Whether you're planning a trip to Brazil or simply want to enhance your language skills, this guide is designed to help you navigate the vibrant world of Brazilian shopping with ease.

Introduction

In this guide, we will cover essential terms and phrases that you might encounter while shopping in Brazil. You'll learn how to ask for prices, discuss sizes, and understand common expressions used by shopkeepers and customers. By the end of this guide, you'll be well-equipped to handle a variety of shopping situations with confidence and ease.

Purchase

Comprar | to buy / purchase
Vender | to sell / purchase
Querer | Want
Quanto que custa? | How much does it cost?
Preço | Price
Pagamento | payment
Barato | Cheap
Caro | Expensive

Eu quero comprar isso
I want to buy this
Eu não quero comprar isso
I don't want to buy this

By mastering this essential vocabulary, you not only improve your language proficiency but also gain confidence and independence in various purchasing scenarios!
