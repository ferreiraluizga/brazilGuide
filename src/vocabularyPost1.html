Start at the beginning: Greeting words

Greetings are an essential part of any language, serving as the foundation for polite interaction and social connection.

Introduction

In Brazilian Portuguese, a rich variety of expressions and phrases are used to greet others, each reflecting the warmth and friendliness that characterize Brazilian culture. This content will introduce you to the common greetings in Brazilian Portuguese, providing you with the tools to make a positive first impression and engage effectively in everyday conversations. Whether you are meeting someone for the first time, catching up with a friend, or simply passing by a neighbor, mastering these greetings will enhance your communication skills and deepen your understanding of Brazilian social etiquette. Let's dive into the world of Brazilian Portuguese greetings and discover how to start every conversation on the right note.

Greetings

Olá (formal) | Hello
Oi (informal and usual) | Hi
Nome | Name
Prazer | Pleasure
Eu sou | I am
Eu estou | I am

Formal
Olá, meu nome é Emma, prazer
Hello, my name is Emma, mice to meet you
Olá Emma, prazer em te conhecer, meu nome é João
Hello Emma, nice to meet you, my name is João
Usual
Oi, eu sou a Emma, prazer
Hi, I'm Emma, nice to meet you
Oi Emma, prazer em te conhecer, eu sou o João
Hi Emma, nice to meet you, I'm João
Como você está?
How are you?
Estou bem Emma, e você?
I'm fine Emma, and you?

Now you've just learned the basic greetings, congratulations!
