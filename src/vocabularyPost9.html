Address

Understanding how to identify and articulate an address is an essential skill, especially when navigating a new language.

Introduction

In Brazilian Portuguese, there are specific words and phrases that are typically used to denote different components of an address. This guide aims to introduce you to these key terms, helping you become more familiar with the vocabulary needed to accurately identify and communicate addresses in Brazilian Portuguese.

Address

Rua | Street
Avenida | Avenue
Cidade | City
Estado | State
País | Country

A small content, but extremely important. Even though it is basic, you are ready to start further studies!
