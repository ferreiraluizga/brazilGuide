Meals

Brazilian cuisine is renowned for its vibrant flavors, unique ingredients, and cultural significance. Understanding the names of different meals in Portuguese will not only enhance your culinary vocabulary but also give you a deeper appreciation of Brazilian culture.

Introduction

Whether you're a language enthusiast, a culinary explorer, or planning a trip to Brazil, knowing the specific terms for meals can be incredibly useful. From breakfast to dinner, and the delightful snacks in between, this guide will walk you through the essential meal names in Brazilian Portuguese.

Meals

Café | Coffee (Alone it has the meaning of coffee)
Café da manhã | Breakfast
Almoço | Lunch
Jantar | Dinner

A small content, but extremely important. Even though it is basic, you are ready to start further studies!
