Be respectful: Kind words

In any language, the use of polite expressions plays a vital role in effective communication and fostering positive relationships.

Introduction

This content aims to introduce these polite expressions, explain their appropriate usage, and provide examples to help you incorporate them naturally into your conversations. By mastering these simple yet powerful words, you can enhance your communication skills and build stronger, more respectful connections with Portuguese speakers.

Kind words

Obrigado/Obrigada (Varies by gender) | Thank you
Desculpa | Sorry
Com licença | Excuse me
Por favor | Please

Me desculpe e obrigada pela consideração
I'm sorry and thank you for your consideration
Com licença, você pode me dar essa informação? Por favor
Excuse me, can you give me this information? Please

Now you've just learned the kind words, congratulations!
